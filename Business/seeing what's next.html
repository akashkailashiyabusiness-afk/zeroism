Seeing What’s Next

By Clayton M. Christensen, Scott D. Anthony & Erik Roth

Using the Theories of Innovation to Predict Industry Change

परिचय: भविष्य देखना सीखना (Introduction: Learning to See the Future) 🔭

Clayton Christensen "The Innovator's Dilemma" ke liye mashhoor hain, jahan unhone disruptive innovation ki theory di thi. "Seeing What's Next" us kaam ko aage badhati hai. Ye kitaab sirf yeh nahi batati ki disruption *kyun* hota hai, balki yeh ek practical framework deti hai ki aap kaise *predict* kar sakte hain ki aapki industry mein agla bada change (disruption) kahan se aa sakta hai aur kaunsi companies usmein jeetengi ya haarengi. 🤔💡

The core idea is ki innovation random nahi hai. Agar aap sahi theories (lenses) use karein, toh aap industry changes ke patterns ko samajh sakte hain aur anticipate kar sakte hain ki aage kya hone wala hai. It's like having special glasses 👓 jo aapko future ke signals dekhne mein madad karti hain. Ye kitaab un theories ko explain karti hai aur unhe use karke alag-alag industries (jaise aviation, semiconductors, education, healthcare) ka analysis karti hai taaki aap seekh sakein ki in tools ko apni industry mein kaise apply karna hai. Matlab, "kal kya hoga" yeh dekhne ki koshish karna.

मुख्य सिद्धांत: इनोवेशन के लेंस (Core Theories: The Lenses of Innovation) 👓렌즈

Kitaab kuch key theories par based hai jinko samajhna zaroori hai future ko dekhne ke liye:

1. Disruptive Innovation Theory (विघटनकारी इनोवेशन सिद्धांत) 📉📈

Yeh Christensen ki original theory hai. Iske do main types hain:

  • Low-End Disruption: Yahan ek naya entrant market ke sabse kam demanding customers ko target karta hai ek "good enough" product ke saath jo sasta aur simple hota hai. Incumbents (badi, established companies) in customers ko ignore karte hain kyunki wahan profit margin kam hota hai. Dheere-dheere, disruptor apni quality improve karta hai aur upar ke market segments mein move karta hai, incumbents ko displace karte hue. Example: Nirma ne low-end market mein enter karke HUL jaise giants ko challenge kiya tha. 🧼
  • New-Market Disruption: Yahan ek innovator aise customers ko target karta hai jo pehle product/service ko afford ya access hi nahi kar paa rahe the (non-consumers). Woh ek simpler, more convenient, ya affordable solution offer karte hain jo ek naya market create karta hai. Example: Personal computers ne un logon ke liye computing laya jo mainframes use nahi kar sakte the. 💻➡️🏠

Is theory ko samajhna zaroori hai kyunki yeh batati hai ki threats aksar neeche se ya bahar se aati hain, jahan incumbents dekh nahi rahe hote. Aapko dekhna hai ki kaun low-end ya non-consumption ko target kar raha hai. 👀

2. Jobs to Be Done Theory (काम पूरा कराने का सिद्धांत) 🛠️🎯

Yeh theory kehti hai ki customers products ya services nahi khareedte, woh unhe 'hire' karte hain ek specific 'job' (kaam) poora karne ke liye. Agar aap samajh jaayein ki customer asal mein kya 'job' karwana chahta hai, toh aap behtar solutions design kar sakte hain.

Example: Log subah milkshake kyun khareedte hain? Shayad 'job' yeh hai ki unhe commute ke time kuch engaging chahiye jo pet bhi bhare aur ek haath se consume kiya ja sake. Is job ko samajhkar, aap milkshake ko behtar bana sakte hain (thicker, longer lasting) ya completely alag solution (jaise smoothie ya breakfast bar) offer kar sakte hain. 🥤➡️🚗

Future dekhne ke liye, aapko sochna hoga ki customers ke 'unmet jobs' kya hain ya kaunse 'jobs' naye solutions se behtar kiye ja sakte hain. Product features ke bajaye customer ke 'job' par focus karo.

3. Resources, Processes, and Values (RPV) Framework (संसाधन, प्रक्रियाएं, और मूल्य फ्रेमवर्क) 🧱⚙️🧭

Yeh framework explain karta hai ki ek company kya kar sakti hai aur kya nahi (its capabilities).

  • Resources: Yeh tangible (factories, cash, people) aur intangible (brand, technology) assets hain. Resources flexible hote hain, unhe alag-alag kaamo mein lagaya jaa sakta hai. 💰🏭
  • Processes: Yeh woh tareeke hain jinse company resources ko use karke value create karti hai (e.g., product development process, manufacturing process, sales process). Processes kam flexible hote hain, woh specific tasks ke liye optimize kiye jaate hain. 🔄⚙️
  • Values: Yeh woh criteria hain jinse company priorities set karti hai aur decisions leti hai (e.g., profit margin requirements, target customer size). Values sabse kam flexible hote hain aur define karte hain ki company kaunse opportunities pursue karegi aur kaunse ignore. ✅❌🎯

RPV framework important hai kyunki yeh explain karta hai ki incumbents (badi companies) aksar disruptive innovations ko pursue kyun *nahi kar paati*, bhale hi unke paas resources hon. Unke processes aur values unhe low-margin ya small-market opportunities ko reject karne par majboor karte hain. Future dekhne ke liye, aapko na sirf apni company ka RPV samajhna hoga, balki competitors (especially potential disruptors) ka RPV bhi analyse karna hoga.

4. Value Chain Evolution (मूल्य श्रृंखला का विकास)⛓️➡️💸

Yeh theory batati hai ki industry value chains (jahan different companies different steps handle karti hain - design, manufacturing, distribution etc.) kaise evolve hoti hain. Shuru mein, jab product performance kaafi nahi hoti, toh companies *integrated* hoti hain (sab kuch khud karti hain) taaki performance optimize kar sakein. Jab product 'good enough' ho jaata hai, toh industry *modularize* hone lagti hai – alag-alag companies value chain ke specific parts mein specialize karti hain. Isse cost, speed, aur customization improve hoti hai.

Profit (value) bhi value chain mein shift hota hai. Aksar, integrated players se specialized, modular players ki taraf value migrate hoti hai. Future dekhne ke liye, aapko dekhna hoga ki aapki industry ki value chain kis taraf move kar rahi hai – integration ya modularity – aur paisa kahan ban raha hai. 💰❓

सिग्नलों की पहचान करना: भविष्य के संकेत (Identifying the Signals: Signs of the Future) 📡🚦

In theories ka use karke, aap kuch specific signals dhoondh sakte hain jo batate hain ki industry mein change aane wala hai:

  • Who are the Non-Consumers?: Kaun log hain jo abhi market ko use nahi kar paa rahe hain affordability, accessibility ya skill ki kami ki wajah se? Yahan New-Market Disruption ka potential hai. 🤔🤷‍♂️
  • Is the Low End Being Ignored?: Kya incumbents market ke sabse kam demanding customers ko over-serve kar rahe hain (unhe features de rahe hain jinke liye woh pay nahi karna chahte) aur ignore kar rahe hain? Yahan Low-End Disruption ka potential hai. 👇😠
  • What Job is the Customer Trying to Get Done?: Kya customers current solutions se frustrate hain? Kya unka real 'job' aapse behtar koi aur samajh raha hai? 💡🎯
  • Can Disruptors Make Money at Lower Prices?: Kya koi naya entrant hai jiska business model (RPV) use kam price par bhi profit kamane deta hai, jahan incumbents nahi kama sakte? 💸📉
  • Is the Basis of Competition Shifting?: Kya industry mein competition pehle performance par tha, aur ab reliability, convenience, ya price par shift ho raha hai? Yeh signal hai ki disruption ka mauka ban raha hai. 🏎️➡️🚲
  • Is the Value Chain Dis-integrating?: Kya industry mein specialized players emerge ho rahe hain jo pehle integrated companies ke kaam kar rahe hain? Value (profit) unki taraf shift ho sakti hai. ⛓️💔➡️🧩
  • Are Incumbents Moving Upmarket?: Kya badi companies consistently high-end customers par focus kar rahi hain aur lower segments ko chhod rahi hain? Yeh disruptors ke liye jagah bana raha hai. 🏰⬆️

In signals ko consistently track karke, aap industry changes ko pehle se sense kar sakte hain. Yeh crystal ball 🔮 nahi hai, lekin yeh aapko ek educated guess lagane mein madad karta hai.

इनोवेशन को लागू करना: रणनीति बनाना (Applying the Insights: Making Strategy) 🗺️✍️

Sirf future dekhna kaafi nahi hai, us par act karna bhi zaroori hai. Kitaab kuch practical advice deti hai:

  • Use the Theories as Lenses: Apni industry aur competitors ko in theories ke through dekhein. Regular strategic discussions mein in concepts ka istemaal karein.
  • Assess Your Own RPV: Apni company ki capabilities (aur limitations) ko honestly evaluate karein. Kya aapka current RPV aapko naye opportunities capture karne dega?
  • Create Separate Business Units (Sometimes): Agar koi disruptive opportunity aapke main business ke values aur processes se clash karti hai, toh use pursue karne ke liye ek alag unit banane par consider karein, jiska apna RPV ho. 🏢➡️🌱
  • Acquire Strategically: Agar aap khud disruption nahi kar sakte, toh disruptive potential wali chhoti companies ko acquire karne par sochein. Lekin unhe integrate karte waqt unke RPV ko destroy na karein. 🤝💡
  • Focus on the Job, Not Just the Customer: Customer demographic data se aage badhkar unke underlying 'Jobs to Be Done' ko samjhein.
  • Don't Dismiss Small Opportunities: Disruptions aksar chhote markets se shuru hoti hain. Unhe sirf isliye ignore na karein kyunki woh aaj chhote hain. Woh kal bade ho sakte hain. 🌱➡️🌳

Essentially, strategy making ek continuous process hona chahiye jismein aap in theories ka use karke environment ko scan karte hain, signals identify karte hain, aur apni company ko position karte hain taaki woh changes se benefit kar sake, na ki victim bane.

मुख्य सीख और निष्कर्ष (Key Takeaways and Conclusion) ✅

"Seeing What's Next" innovation aur strategy ke students aur practitioners ke liye ek powerful toolkit hai. Key takeaways hain:

  • Innovation is Predictable: Industry change random nahi hai. Sahi theories ka use karke iske patterns ko samjha ja sakta hai. 🧩
  • Use Multiple Lenses: Disruption Theory, Jobs-to-be-Done, RPV Framework, aur Value Chain analysis – in sabko ek saath use karke zyada accurate picture milti hai. 👓
  • Look for Signals: Non-consumption, ignored low-end, shifting basis of competition, RPV mismatches, value chain shifts – yeh sab important signals hain. 📡
  • Understand Capabilities (RPV): Sirf resources nahi, processes aur values bhi determine karte hain ki company kya kar sakti hai. Yeh incumbents ke liye challenge aur disruptors ke liye opportunity ho sakti hai. 🧱⚙️🧭
  • Focus on the Job: Customer ke underlying need ('job') ko samajhna innovation ki key hai. 🎯
  • Strategy is Foresight + Action: Future ko anticipate karna pehla step hai, lekin uske according apni strategy ko adapt karna crucial hai. 🔭➡️🏃‍♂️

Yeh kitaab aapko sikhata hai ki sirf past data par rely karne ke bajaye, robust theories ka use karke future possibilities ko systematically explore kaise karein. Yeh aapko ek zyada proactive aur insightful strategist banne mein madad karti hai, jo sirf react nahi karta, balki anticipate karta hai aur shape karta hai ki 'what's next'. Ek essential read for anyone trying to navigate today's dynamic business environment. 🚀🌍
