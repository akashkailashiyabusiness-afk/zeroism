Don't Die by Bryan Johnson

A visionary dialogue on conquering mortality through science and technology.

Overview

"Don't Die" by Bryan Johnson is a unique blend of science fiction and philosophical exploration, published in 2023. Presented as a Socratic dialogue among twelve characters—each representing a facet of Johnson’s psyche—the book unfolds on the final day of a character named Scribe, who gathers friends atop Mount Kilimanjaro to devise a plan for humanity’s future. Drawing from Johnson’s real-life Project Blueprint and his "Don't Die" movement, it explores radical ideas about longevity, artificial intelligence, and human evolution, aiming to inspire a blueprint for overcoming death and aligning humanity with technology and planetary health.

Key Concepts

Longevity Through Technology

Johnson advocates using data-driven health (e.g., his Blueprint protocol) and programmable biology to extend human life, envisioning a future where aging is optional.

AI and Human Alignment

The book proposes integrating artificial intelligence with human consciousness to enhance capabilities and ensure survival, addressing existential risks like misaligned AI.

Collective Evolution

It calls for a global movement to rethink humanity’s trajectory, balancing individual health with planetary and technological harmony.

Lessons

  • Embrace Radical Health: Commit to extreme, evidence-based health practices (e.g., diet, plasma therapy) to push the boundaries of human lifespan.
  • Think Beyond the Self: Address personal mortality while contributing to a collective strategy for humanity’s future.
  • Harness AI Wisely: Use AI as a partner to amplify human potential, not a threat, by aligning its goals with ours.
  • Act Now: Don’t wait for societal norms to shift—start experimenting with life-extension and tech integration today.

Life Examples

Tracking Health Metrics

Adopt Johnson’s Blueprint approach by monitoring biomarkers daily (e.g., sleep, diet) to optimize personal health, as he does with over 100 metrics.

Building an AI Assistant

Create a personal AI to manage tasks and health data, mirroring Johnson’s vision of tech-augmented living.

Joining a Movement

Participate in or start a "Don't Die" community to share longevity strategies, inspired by Johnson’s global call to action.
