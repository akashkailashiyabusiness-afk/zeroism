2. The 5 Whys Framework

Uncover the real reason behind recurring problems–fast.

How to use it:

Ask "why" five times in a row to uncover the root cause.

When to use it:

You're dealing with recurring performance problems.

PROBLEM We missed our revenue target this quarter.
WHY 1Sales came in 20% below forecast.
WHY 2The new product failed to gain traction.
WHY 3Our marketing didn't connect with the right audience.
WHY 4We launched without validating the messaging.
WHY 5We prioritized investor optics over go-to-market readiness.

3. Decision Tree Analysis

How to use it:

  • Draw out each decision path.
  • Attach outcomes, risks, and potential returns.
  • Select based on clarity.

When to use it:

You're making complex decisions with multiple variables or trade-offs.

Who should lead our product launch execution?
OPTION A
Proven but Overbooked Team
RISK

OUTCOME

OUTCOME
