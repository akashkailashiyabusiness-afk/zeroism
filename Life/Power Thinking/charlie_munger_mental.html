Mental Models Guide

Core Mental Models

Inversion: Start with the worst possible outcome and work backwards. What would guarantee failure?

Incentives: Follow the incentives. People respond to incentives, often in unexpected ways.

Circle of Competence: Know what you know and what you don't. Stay within your expertise or acknowledge limitations.

Advanced Models

Lollapalooza Effect: When multiple psychological tendencies act together, they can create extreme outcomes.

Second-Order Thinking: Consider not just immediate consequences, but consequences of consequences.

Systems Thinking: Everything is connected. Look for feedback loops and unintended consequences.

Common Biases to Watch For

Confirmation Bias: Seeking information that confirms existing beliefs while ignoring contradictory evidence.

Anchoring: Over-relying on the first piece of information encountered.

Sunk Cost Fallacy: Continuing a behavior because of previously invested resources.
