Your Personalized Health Plan 🤖

Diet Plan 🥗

  • Calorie Surplus: Aim for 2,500–2,800 calories daily to support mass gain (500–800 kcal above maintenance). Focus on nutrient-dense foods for health. 🍽️
  • Protein-Rich Foods: Consume 1.6–2.2g protein/kg body weight (72–99g daily). Include lentils (dal), chickpeas, paneer, tofu, and eggs (if non-vegetarian). Add 1–2 scoops of plant-based protein powder (e.g., pea protein). 🥚🥜
  • Complex Carbs: Use brown rice, quinoa, oats, or millets (ragi, jowar) for sustained energy. Aim for 4–6g carbs/kg (180–270g daily). Try 1 cup ragi porridge or 2 multigrain rotis per meal. 🍚
  • Healthy Fats: Include 0.8–1g fat/kg (36–45g daily) from almonds, peanuts, mustard oil, or ghee (in moderation). Add 1 tbsp flaxseed oil to smoothies. 🥑
  • Indian Superfoods: Incorporate amla (vitamin C), turmeric (anti-inflammatory), and moringa (micronutrients) for health benefits. Try 1 tsp amla powder daily. 🌿
  • Meal Plan Example: Breakfast (2 moong dal cheelas, 1 glass milk with protein powder), Mid-Morning (handful almonds, 1 banana), Lunch (1 cup chana masala, 2 multigrain rotis, 1 cup veggies), Evening (1 cup sprout salad, green tea), Dinner (1 cup paneer bhurji, 1 cup brown rice, 1 cup dal). 🥗

Exercise Plan 💪

  • Strength Training: 4–5 days/week, 45–60 min. Focus on compound exercises (e.g., squats, push-ups, pull-ups) to build muscle. Start with bodyweight: 3 sets of 10–12 push-ups, 3x10 bodyweight squats. 🏋️‍♂️
  • Yoga for Flexibility: 2 days/week, 20–30 min surya namaskar (10 rounds) and poses like warrior II, tree pose to improve mobility and recovery. 🧘
  • Cardio: 2–3 days/week, 20 min brisk walking or cycling (heart rate 120–150 bpm) to boost stamina without burning excess calories. 🚴
  • Progressive Overload: Gradually increase weights or reps (e.g., add dumbbells after 4 weeks). Use affordable resistance bands if gym access is limited. 💪
  • Indian Adaptation: Practice yoga at home or join community fitness groups for low-cost workouts. Walk in local parks for cardio. 🌳🇮🇳

Lifestyle Tips 😴

  • Sleep: Aim for 7–8 hours nightly in a cool, dark room. Use cotton sheets and limit screen time 1 hour before bed. Practice pranayama (e.g., anulom vilom) for relaxation. 🛌
  • Stress Management: Meditate 10 min daily using apps like Calm or simple deep breathing. Join local yoga classes for mindfulness. 🧘
  • Hydration: Drink 2.5–3 liters of water daily. Add lemon or mint for flavor. 💧
  • Indian Adaptation: Use affordable cooling mats, practice ayurvedic rituals like oil pulling, and maintain a consistent sleep schedule. 🌿🇮🇳

Warnings ⚠️

  • Experimental Therapies: Avoid Blueprint's gene therapy and stem cell injections due to unproven safety and high costs. Stick to evidence-based practices like diet and exercise. 🧬
  • Supplements: Limit to basic multivitamins and protein powders; consult a doctor before using costly Blueprint supplements (e.g., NAD+ boosters). 💊
