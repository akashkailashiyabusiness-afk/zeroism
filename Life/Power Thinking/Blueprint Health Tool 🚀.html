Blueprint for India 🇮🇳

Adapting the Blueprint protocol for the Indian context is crucial for affordability and sustainability. Here's how you can do it.

Diet Adaptation

Focus on local, seasonal vegetables. Replace exotic berries with amla and jamun. Use lentils (dal), chickpeas (chana), and paneer as primary protein sources. Incorporate traditional spices like turmeric, ginger, and cumin.

Exercise Adaptation

Embrace traditional practices like Yoga and Surya Namaskar for flexibility and strength. Utilize community parks for calisthenics (bodyweight exercises) and running. Resistance bands are a low-cost alternative to weights.

Supplement Adaptation

Prioritize whole foods over expensive supplements. Chyawanprash, Ashwagandha, Tulsi, and Triphala are powerful Ayurvedic supplements that are both affordable and effective for overall wellness.
