Pomodoro Technique

Inventor: Francesco Cirillo

Break work into 25-minute focused intervals with 5-minute breaks. After 4 cycles, take a longer 15-30 minute break.

2 Minute Rule

If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it immediately. If longer, delegate or defer.

MSCW Method

Prioritize by: Must-Have, Should-Have, Could-Have, Won't-Have.

  • Core functionality (Must-Have)
  • Important feature (Should-Have)
  • Nice-to-have (Could-Have)
  • Future enhancement (Won't-Have)

5/25 Rule

Focus only on your top 5 priorities and ignore the rest.

  1. Complete product launch
  2. Hire new team member
  3. Develop marketing strategy
  4. Improve customer service
  5. Financial planning
