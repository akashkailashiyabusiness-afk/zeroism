# इंटरनेट का असली खजाना — क्यूरेटेड वेब रिसोर्सेज (Curated Web Resources)

यह फ़ाइल आपके सभी HTML खज़ाना फ़ाइलों और नए जोड़े गए **GitHub**, **TEDx**, और **Quora** के महत्वपूर्ण रिसोर्सेज का एकीकृत (Unified) डेटाबेस है।

## निर्देश
नीचे दी गई श्रेणियों में से प्रत्येक रिसोर्स के सामने उसका सीधा लिंक, विवरण, और उसे उपयोग करने का ठोस कारण दिया गया है।

## Forums & Communities

कुल रिसोर्सेज: **10**

### 🔗 [Hacker News](https://news.ycombinator.com)
- **विवरण (Description):** Y Combinator dwara chalaya gaya forum. Duniya ke top founders, engineers aur scientists yahan daily news discuss karte hain.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Agar aapko tech industry ki asli pulse janni hai, toh HN se better kuch nahi. No memes, pure high-intellect discussion.

---

### 🔗 [LessWrong](https://www.lesswrong.com)
- **विवरण (Description):** A community dedicated to improving human reasoning and understanding cognitive biases. Deep focus on AI alignment.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Inke articles aur discussions aapke sochne ka tarika (Mental Models) completely badal denge. Hardcore logic.

---

### 🔗 [Lobste.rs](https://lobste.rs)
- **विवरण (Description):** A highly curated, invite-only community for computing and software development.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Reddit ke programming subreddits ab basic ho gaye hain. Lobsters par actual hardcore engineers system design discuss karte hain.

---

### 🔗 [Tildes](https://tildes.net)
- **विवरण (Description):** A non-profit, ad-free alternative to Reddit focused on high-quality discussion and extreme moderation.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Reddit ka purana, better version jahan log actually long-form thoughtful comments likhte hain, trolls nahi.

---

### 🔗 [Stack Exchange Network](https://stackexchange.com/sites)
- **विवरण (Description):** Stack Overflow ke alava, Inke paas Math, Physics, Cryptography, aur Reverse Engineering ke 170+ expert Q&A sites hain.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Quora par ab bots aur fake stories hain. Stack Exchange par real experts point-to-point technical answers dete hain.

---

### 🔗 [Stack Overflow](https://stackoverflow.com)
- **विवरण (Description):** Programming ka real judicial system. Real devs, real errors, real solutions.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Kisi bhi technical problem ka answer yahan pehle aata hai. Google pe jo aata hai, woh yahi se aata hai.
- **टैग्स (Tags):** `coding`, `debugging`, `technical`

---

### 🔗 [Lesswrong](https://lesswrong.com)
- **विवरण (Description):** AI safety, rationality, epistemology, decision theory ka deepest community online.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Yahan log seriously sochte hain. Eliezer Yudkowsky ne yahan hi AI risk explain kiya tha saalon pehle.
- **टैग्स (Tags):** `rationality`, `AI`, `philosophy`

---

### 🔗 [Quora Spaces (Niche)](https://quora.com/spaces)
- **विवरण (Description):** General Quora nahi — specific Spaces main actual practitioners likhte hain.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Surface Quora garbage hai. Deep Spaces main IIT professors, ex-VCs, actual doctors hain.
- **टैग्स (Tags):** `experts`, `stories`, `diverse`

---

### 🔗 [Quora Space: The Philosophy Space](https://www.quora.com/q/philosophy)
- **विवरण (Description):** Curated Q&A community discussing existentialism, epistemology, and ethics.
- **क्यों उपयोगी है (Why it's a gem):** Contains high-quality, long-form explanations of complex philosophical systems.
- **टैग्स (Tags):** `quora`, `philosophy`, `ethics`, `rationality`

---

### 🔗 [Quora Space: Macroeconomics & Central Banking](https://www.quora.com/topic/Macroeconomics)
- **विवरण (Description):** Discussions on fiat money, federal reserve audits, inflation, and monetary policies.
